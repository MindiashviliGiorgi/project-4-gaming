About website

On this website you can purchase computer games. You can register your account then log in and log out anytime you want. Also you'll see a 'navbar' which can help you to search the product by it's price, name or year of release. When you click on the product name you will get the full information about the game. If you want to purchase a product you will need to log in first then search for the game and add it to cart so you can pay. Web is full responsive The idea and design of the site is entirely my own
